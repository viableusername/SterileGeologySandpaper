Upgrades

fracture reality

fracturing reality makes things update every tick rather than in set intervals

before the fracture reality upgrade, your operators update once every x amount of time but after the fracturing they will update every tick (50ms aka 20t/second) so if your incriminator increments every second, then it will increment 1/20th of what it normally increments every 20th of a second, for something like the incriminator this doesn't make much of a difference but it will give a boost to some operators like the multiplicator, if we do 1 x2 once every second then it becomes 2, 4, 8, etc. but if we do it every 1/20th of a second then it becomes 2.65, 7.03, 18.68, etc
